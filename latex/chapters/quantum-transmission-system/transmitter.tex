\section{Transmitter}

Let us consider a tensor product of $n$ coherent states
\begin{equation}
	\ket{\alpha_1,\dots,\alpha_n}
	=
	\ket{\alpha_1}
	\otimes
	\ket{\alpha_2}
	\otimes
	\cdots
	\otimes
	\ket{\alpha_n}
\end{equation}
with the coherent state 
\begin{equation}
	\ket{\alpha_j}
	=
	\hat{D}_j[\alpha_j]
	\ket{0}
	=
	e^{-\overline{n}_j/2}
	\exp\left\{
		\int\dd{p}
		\alpha_j
		\delta^{(1)}(p-p_0)
		\hat{a}_j^\dagger(p)
	\right\}
	\ket{0}
\end{equation}
having mean photon number $\overline{n}_j=\expval{\hat{N}}{\alpha_j}$ and fundamental frequency $p_0$.
