\subsection{Relativistic field theory}

The Lagrangian of the Maxwell field $A^\mu(t,\vb{x})$ is~\cite[p.~339]{Srednicki2007}.
\begin{equation}
	\mathcal{L}
	=
	\frac{1}{2}
	(\partial_\mu A_\nu)
	\left(
		\partial^\nu A^\mu
		-
		\partial^\mu A^\nu
	\right)
	.
\end{equation}
